Plain Rice

Ingredients

  • 3 cups water
  • 2 cups rice

Directions

  1. Bring water to boil
  2. Add salt
  3. Add rice
  4. Lower heat all the way
  5. Let cook 20 minutes without moving lid
  6. After 20 mins turn off heat and move rice
  7. Cover rice and let rest for 5 minutes before serving
